It started, as most big ideas do, with a late-night conversation and a half-finished bottle of wine.

We had been talking about New Zealand for years. The scenery. The friendly people. The fact that it was about as far from our everyday lives as a place can be. But with two kids under ten, the 17-hour flight felt like a wall we'd never actually climb.

Then one evening we just decided: we're going.

What followed was four months of planning, a lot of spreadsheet rows, and one very long flight that somehow wasn't as bad as we feared. Three weeks later, we flew home with full memory cards, tired kids, and a firm conviction that New Zealand with children is not just doable - it's spectacular.

This site is everything we wish we had found when we were planning.
